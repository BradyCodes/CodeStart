CodeStart?

I created CodeStart because as a young developer, I noticed that the hampstead/manchester area had a shortage of teenagers in technology, specifically programming. I want to teach kids the valuble skill of programming, and maybe even more valuble, the ability to think pragmatically and to be intuitive.
